# Supported Games
This is a list of games that are verified to be working
> Note: As of Mar 20 2026

## Known Limitations
This list may not be accurate as of the time of writing I don't have all of the games installed
but it is highly likely that other games from Ubisoft and Rockstar work just fine as well.

Some games that do not work due to them using a different path or something else are:
- Anno series (Saves in documents folder)
- GTA 3 
- Unreal Games that do not use the built-in savegame functionality

These are relatively simple to fix and will be addressed in future releases, but for now this is a
verified list of games from my testing, contributions are welcome!
